  • To travelers

Personal data monetization

For each permission to use the data, the user gets tokens, which he can exchange for money or a tourist service

It is as simple to organize a trip as to get Uber

Services already have info which countries the user have visited, what cuisine he likes, what kind of sport he is into. A personal tour will be designed on these data as a construction kit.

TravelChain for Business

Savings on advertising and access to clients at an early decision-making stage

Artificial intelligence will tell you about the user's wishes before he himself realizes them.

Companies will stop wasting money on mass advertising and will start making personal offers in a few clicks.

For instance:

The hotel manager sees that the user is planning to visit Madrid, and knows that he is a vegetarian and practices yoga.

So, he sends him an offer: a room with a vegetarian breakfast, which is already included in the price. The hotel is situated next to a yoga center.
