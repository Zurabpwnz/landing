TravelChain brings benefits both to business and to end users
To travelers
Personal data
monetization
For each permission to use the data, the user gets tokens, which he can exchange for money or a tourist service
It is as simple to
organize a trip as to get Uber
Services already have info which countries the user have visited, what cuisine he likes, what kind of sport he is into. A personal tour will be designed on these data as a construction kit.
For Business
Savings on advertising and access to clients at an early decision-making stage.
Artificial intelligence will tell you about the user's wishes before he himself realizes them.
Companies will stop wasting money on mass advertising and will start making personal offers in a few clicks.
For instance:
The hotel manager sees that the user is planning to visit Madrid,
and knows that he is a vegetarian and practices yoga.
So, he sends him an offer: a room with a vegetarian breakfast,
which is already included in the price. The hotel is situated next to a yoga center.
Increase of profit due to refusal of commission to online agencies
Companies will be able to sell their services directly to customers without paying high commissions to intermediaries.
Getting recommendations from opinion leaders
Services will be able to analyze potential customers and identify people with high status with a lot of followers.
For instance:
A person with more than 100k subscribers in Instagram booked a hotel. The hotel makes him a discount or special offer - and receives a review that affects the opinions of thousands of users.
To developers
Developers can use TravelChain for their projects and experiments.
They can also sell processed information to companies and advertising platforms.
Here are some examples of services that can be created using TravelChain.
For instance:
  • Map applications
  • Guides
  • Notification systems
  • Widgets
  • Specialized apps
  • AI services
    For example, Aigents service, based on your activities in social networks, identifies your followers, opinion leaders, people with similar interests.
